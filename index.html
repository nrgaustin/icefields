How this iteration works

Real FIT data, lighter visual clutter

The browser decodes the original Garmin files directly. Photo markers are clustered into moments rather than covering the route, and the timeline retains every photograph as a fine pink tick so dense areas remain discoverable.

ChartsSelectableElevation, grade, speed, HR, power and cadence share one aligned axis.
